# Social Media Content Ideas: The Artisan Corporate Gifting Co. (Instagram/Pinterest)

This document outlines content ideas for Instagram and Pinterest, focusing on visual appeal, storytelling, and highlighting the unique selling propositions of handmade corporate gifts.

---

## General Content Pillars:

1.  **Product Showcase:** High-quality photos/videos of finished products, emphasizing craftsmanship and customization.
2.  **Behind-the-Scenes:** Glimpses into the making process, showing the artisan touch and quality materials.
3.  **Customization in Action:** Examples of branded gifts, personalized items, and client logos.
4.  **Lifestyle & Impact:** Showcasing gifts in a professional setting, or recipients enjoying them, highlighting the positive impact.
5.  **Sustainability & Sourcing:** Visuals of eco-friendly materials, ethical sourcing, and sustainable packaging.
6.  **Client Testimonials/Case Studies:** Visuals paired with quotes from satisfied corporate clients.

---
